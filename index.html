Mass Spectrometry and Automation

At Highland Innovations, we believe that Mass Spectrometry is an important tool for identifying and solving problems in varying
industries. We specialize in interpreting mass spectra from various samples and creating solutions using AI and other tools.

Software

MICROID
MicroID is a software tool developed by Highland Innovations for analyzing infectious microorganisms using MALDI-TOF spectral data from patients. MicroID evaluates the spectral quality of MALDI-TOF spectra and provides matching scores to correctly identify the microorganism species.
The intellectual property of MicroID is protected by copyright (US Software Copyright Reg. Tx 8-669-594).

NOSID
NosID is software for diagnosing cancer from MALDI-TOF spectra. It uses mass spectra from blood samples of patients to detect early signs of cancer.
Through different categorizations and algorithms, NosID has been successful in the early diagnosis of ovarian and breast cancer.
The use of NosID may be expanded for early diagnosis of other types of cancers such as stomach cancer, pancreatic cancer, etc.
The intellectual property of NosID is protected by copyright (US Software Copyright Reg. Txu 2-139-709).
